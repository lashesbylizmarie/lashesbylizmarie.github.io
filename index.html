first time booking?

To book your appointment please text (407) 797-8453 or dm on instagram @lizmarie.lashes

Please leave your name, preferred method of contact and the lash style you would like to book for.

If you have any questions or appointment time requests feel free to message me.

Please read through the following policies and select the service you’d like to receive.

Please make sure you arrive 5 minutes prior to your first appointment to ask any questions and get an idea of what you’re wanting to have done.

Please note: Lash extensions are considered a luxury service and can take anywhere from 2-4 hours for a full set.
